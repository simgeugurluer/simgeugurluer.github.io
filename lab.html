Origin & Context

MUSEO_LOGIC is a strategic simulation tool originating from Dr. Simge Uğurluer’s doctoral dissertation: “New museology and virtual reality applications from the perspective of consumer behavior.”

The model operates at the intersection of New Museology, Experiential Marketing, and Advertising.

The Strategic Logic

By synthesizing Heritage Core, Immersive Medium, and Visitor Segment, the tool generates directives that demonstrate how XR can be used to "advertise" history effectively.
